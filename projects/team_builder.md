🎯 Project: Hackathon Team Builder
Objective

Imagine you're organizing an AI hackathon. Many people apply to join, and you need to create balanced teams.

Each applicant has:

Name
Skills
Years of experience

Your program should:

Store applicants.
Filter applicants who know Python.
Create teams of 2.
Make sure each team has at least one experienced person (>3 years).
Print the teams nicely.
